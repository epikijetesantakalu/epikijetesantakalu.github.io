What's an epikijetesantakalu?

epikijetesantakalu is an nimi sin(new toki pona word) that I coined. It means "Cozumel Raccoon", which is another type of raccoon, different from the common raccoon.

Last time I checked, I was the only Cozumel raccoon speaking toki pona, so I do not need a toki pona name. However, if another Cozumel raccoon started speaking toki pona, I might look for a name.
