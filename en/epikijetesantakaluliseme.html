What's an epikijetesantakalu?

epikijetesantakalu is an nimi sin(new toki pona word) that I coined. It means "Kozumel Raccoon", which is the only other type of raccoon, along with the common raccoon.

Last time I checked, I was the only Kozumel raccoon speaking toki pona, so I do not need a toki pona name. However, if another Kozumel raccoon started speaking toki pona, I might look for a name.
